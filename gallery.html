FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the projects featured in our gallery.

Are these real client projects?

Yes, every photo in this gallery is from an actual installation we've completed for a real client — homes, schools, hotels, hospitals, and community projects across the country. None of these are stock images or renders.

Can I request a setup similar to one in the gallery?

Absolutely. If a project catches your eye, just let us know which one and we'll use it as a reference point to design something similar for your space, adjusting the size and system type to fit your needs and budget.

What's the difference between the system types shown?

Multistorey Gardens and Tower Gardens are compact stacked systems ideal for small spaces, Grow Bags offer a flexible low-cost option, NFT systems are a hydroponic setup suited to higher-volume growing, and Irrigation Kits support automated watering for any of the above. Use the filters above to browse by type.

Do you have projects in my area?

We've completed projects across 10 counties and counting. Even if you don't see your specific area represented in the gallery yet, we deliver and install nationwide, so reach out and we'll confirm availability for your location.

Are these home installs or commercial only?

Both. The gallery includes a mix of home gardens, school projects, and commercial or institutional installations, so you can browse examples close to the scale you're planning, whether that's a balcony setup or a full institutional system.

How do you achieve a 95% satisfaction rate?

It comes down to careful installation, post-install training, and ongoing maintenance support, so clients aren't left figuring things out alone. We follow up after every project to make sure systems are performing as expected.

How much does a system like these cost?

Pricing depends on the system type and scale, ranging from KSh 1,500 for a Multistorey Garden up to KSh 15,000 for an NFT System. Contact us with a project you like from the gallery and we'll give you a personalized quote.

Can I submit my own project for the gallery?

Yes! If you've installed one of our systems and would like to share photos of your setup, send them our way through the contact page — we love featuring client gardens here.
