Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before reaching out or placing an order.

How quickly will you respond to my enquiry?

We aim to reply to every message within 2 hours during business hours, and within 24 hours at most. For the fastest response, message us directly on WhatsApp using the button above.

What's the best way to reach you?

WhatsApp is the quickest way to get a response, especially for quick questions or photos of your space. For detailed project enquiries, the contact form lets us capture all the details we need to prepare an accurate quote.

Can I get a quote before visiting your location?

Yes — share your space dimensions, location, and what you'd like to grow through the form or WhatsApp, and we'll send a tailored quote without you needing to visit us first. A site visit is only needed for large commercial installations.

Do I need to book an appointment to visit you?

Appointments are preferred so our team can prepare a proper demo and answer your questions in detail. Call, WhatsApp, or use the form to schedule a time that works for you before heading to Olekasasi, Ongata Rongai.

Do you deliver outside Nairobi?

Yes, we deliver nationwide across all 47 counties. Let us know your location when you contact us and we'll include a delivery estimate along with your quote.

I already have a system — can I contact you for support?

Of course. Existing customers can reach our support line via call, WhatsApp, or the form for troubleshooting, spare parts, or maintenance visits — just mention your order or installation details so we can assist faster.

I'm enquiring on behalf of a school, hotel, or company — who do I talk to?

Select "Commercial/Institutional Project" in the form's interest dropdown, or mention it when you call or message us, and we'll connect you with our commercial team for a customized proposal.

Is my information kept private when I contact you?

Yes. Details you share through our form, phone, or WhatsApp are used only to respond to your enquiry and are never shared or sold to third parties.
