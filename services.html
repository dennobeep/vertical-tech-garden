FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our installation, training, maintenance, and consultancy services.

What does installation include?

Our on-site installation covers full assembly, plumbing, setup, and system testing before handover. Standard systems are typically installed the same day, while custom setups for schools, hotels, and institutions are scheduled according to project scope. Every installation is finished with clean, organized workmanship.

What does farmer training cover?

Our hands-on training takes you from planting to harvest, including seedling selection and nursery management, nutrient management and pest control, and harvest planning and crop rotation. Sessions can be arranged on-site for individuals, groups, schools, and institutions.

How does maintenance and support work?

We offer bi-monthly system check-ups to keep your setup running at peak performance, along with pump and plumbing maintenance and access to replacement parts. If anything comes up between visits, our team is reachable by phone and WhatsApp for prompt support.

What's included in consultancy?

Our consultancy service provides feasibility studies and site assessments, custom system design and layout, and budget planning with ROI projections. We also support NGOs and institutions with grant and proposal documentation for school feeding and food security initiatives.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, delivery and installation services are available upon request. We serve clients across Kenya and can arrange delivery to your location. Contact us for a delivery quote tailored to your area.

What about commercial projects?

We specialize in both small-scale and large-scale projects. For commercial growers, schools, hotels, hospitals, and institutions, we provide customized solutions including project design, bulk supply, professional installation, training, and ongoing maintenance support.

How much do your services cost?

Service pricing depends on the scope of work — system size, location, and whether it's a standard or custom project. Contact us for a personalized quote covering installation, training, maintenance, or consultancy, and we'll help you find the best solution for your budget.

Can I book training or consultancy without buying a system?

Yes. Training, maintenance, and consultancy services can be booked independently — they aren't limited to customers who purchased a system from us. Many schools and institutions also bring us in for feasibility studies and consultancy before deciding on a system.
