Our Story

Founded in 2021, Vertical Tech Garden is a Kenyan agribusiness dedicated to making food production possible for every household, school, and institution through innovative vertical farming solutions. We believe that everyone — from a family in a Nairobi apartment to a school in rural Kenya — deserves access to fresh, nutritious food grown right where they are.

Our journey began when we saw the disconnect between Kenya's urbanizing population and its shrinking agricultural land. By combining traditional growing knowledge with modern vertical farming techniques, we created systems that let anyone grow food in any space — no matter how small.

5 Years Strong

Growing a Movement

In just 5 years, we have become one of Kenya's trusted providers of multistorey gardening systems, helping families and organizations maximize small spaces while growing fresh, healthy, and organic vegetables. With over 7,000 multistorey gardens installed across more than 35 counties, and 50+ schools and learning institutions served, we've helped thousands of households produce nutritious food at home.

Who is Vertical Tech Garden?

We're a Kenyan agribusiness founded in 2021, dedicated to making food production possible for every household, school, and institution through innovative vertical farming solutions like multistorey gardens, tower gardens, and drip irrigation systems.

How long have you been in business?

We've been growing alongside our customers since 2021 — 5 years and counting. In that time we've become one of Kenya's trusted providers of multistorey gardening systems, with over 7,000 gardens installed nationwide.

Where in Kenya do you operate?

We're present in over 35 counties nationwide, with our base in Olekasasi, Ongata Rongai, Nairobi. Wherever you're located, we can arrange delivery, installation, and ongoing support.

What is your mission?

To promote sustainable agriculture and food security by providing innovative, affordable, and space-saving farming technologies that enable every Kenyan to grow fresh vegetables regardless of land size.

Do you only work with households, or institutions too?

Both. We've empowered thousands of households to grow their own food at home, and we've also partnered with 50+ schools and learning institutions on larger installations, training, and ongoing support.

We don't just sell systems — we partner with you. Every installation is backed by hands-on training, honest advice, and ongoing support, built on the values of sustainability, quality, community, and integrity that guide everything we do.

What's your vision for the future?

To be Kenya's leading provider of vertical farming solutions — empowering communities with sustainable food production technologies that improve nutrition, create livelihoods, and protect the environment.
