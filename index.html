FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about vertical farming and our systems.

What is vertical farming?

Vertical farming is a method of growing crops in stacked layers or vertically inclined surfaces. It maximizes food production in limited spaces by utilizing vertical space rather than horizontal land area. Our systems are designed to make vertical farming accessible and affordable for everyone.

How much space do I need?

Our Multistorey Garden requires approximately 4 square feet of space — that's smaller than a standard doormat! It's perfect for balconies, backyards, terraces, rooftops, and even indoors near a sunny window. For larger systems, we'll help you determine the best setup for your available space.

What crops can I grow?

You can grow a wide variety of crops including kale, spinach, lettuce, sukuma wiki, herbs (cilantro, parsley, basil, mint), strawberries, spring onions, peppers, tomatoes, and many other high-value vegetables. Our systems support year-round production of most leafy greens and vegetables.

Is installation difficult?

Not at all! Our systems are designed for easy DIY installation. Every purchase comes with clear installation guides and growing instructions. We also offer professional installation services upon request to ensure your system is set up perfectly.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, delivery and installation services are available upon request. We serve clients across Kenya and can arrange delivery to your location. Contact us for a delivery quote tailored to your area.

What about commercial projects?

We specialize in both small-scale and large-scale projects. For commercial growers, schools, hotels, hospitals, and institutions, we provide customized solutions including project design, bulk supply, professional installation, training, and ongoing maintenance support.

How much does a system cost?

Our systems range from KSh 1,500 for the Multistorey Garden to KSh 15,000 for the NFT System. The exact cost depends on your chosen system and the number of units required for your space. Contact us for a personalized quote and we'll help you find the best solution for your budget.

Do you provide training?

Yes! We offer comprehensive training and consultancy services for individuals, schools, community groups, and commercial operations. Our training covers system setup, crop management, pest control, and harvesting techniques to ensure your success.
